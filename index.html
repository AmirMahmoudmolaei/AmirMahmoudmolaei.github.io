Selected Projects

Ecological Monitoring · GIS · University of Milan
Impacts of Mining on Bird Populations — Val Sora Quarry
Designed an ecological monitoring protocol using line transects to assess environmental impacts on local Alpine bird communities in the Italian Alps. Applied GIS tools to map spatial data, establish buffer zones around the quarry, and design systematic survey transects.
ArcGIS Line Transect Surveys Spatial Analysis Buffer Zone Mapping
Remote Sensing · GIS · MSc Thesis
Mineral Potential Detection — Nagisan Region, Kerman
Processed Landsat 8 satellite imagery to detect Cu element alterations and map mineral potential in the Nagisan region. Applied AHP and SWARA multi-criteria decision methods for spatial weighting and prospectivity modelling.
ENVI Landsat 8 ArcGIS AHP / SWARA Python
Geophysical Monitoring · BSc Thesis
Time-lapse ERT Monitoring of Tailings Dams
Conducted time-lapse Electrical Resistivity Tomography (ERT) monitoring to track internal changes within mine tailings dams — a critical area for environmental risk management and dam safety assessment.
ERT Geophysical Survey Tailings Management
